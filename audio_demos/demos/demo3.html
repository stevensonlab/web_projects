FIR Loudness Summation:
This uses a 511-tap FIR filter (100dB/octave slope). We alternate every 200ms between a 15% BW reference and your Variable BW selection.

What to notice: The physical power is constant. As you increase the bandwidth, notice the spectrum gets "flatter" to compensate. Once the bandwidth exceeds the Critical Band, the sound will perceptually grow louder.
